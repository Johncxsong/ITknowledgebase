## Expiration of School Email Accounts  

```
Subject: Important Notice: Expiration of School Email Accounts

Dear Graduates,

We hope this message finds you well. As you embark on your post-graduation journey, we would like to inform you of an important update regarding your school email accounts.

Email Expiration Notice

Your school email accounts will expire in 30 days from the date of this notice. After this period, you will no longer have access to your school email and all associated services.

Action Required: Update Your Information

If you have used your school email for any Microsoft services, logos, or other software, please take the following steps to ensure a smooth transition:

Update Your Email: Manually change your email address from your school email to your personal email in all relevant accounts and services.
Backup Important Data: Ensure that you have backed up any important emails or data stored in your school email account.
Contact Information

If you have any questions or need assistance, please do not hesitate to contact us at [Your Contact Information].

We wish you all the best in your future endeavors.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[School Name]
[Contact Information]
```
